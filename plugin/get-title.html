    Plugin execution conditions

    • Automatic trigger, the entry is empty will execute the plugin, otherwise ignored
    • Manually trigger, if the forced access to the title selection is turned on, the plugin must be executed, otherwise the entry title is empty to execute the plugin, otherwise ignored
